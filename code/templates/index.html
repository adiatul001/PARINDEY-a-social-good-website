The Four Regulative Principles

Meat Eating

Meat eating can contribute to heart disease, cancer, obesity, and environmental damage, among other negative health and ecological consequences.

Intoxication

Intoxication can lead to impaired judgment, memory loss, addiction, liver damage, accidents, violence, and even death.

Gambling

Gambling can lead to addiction, financial ruin, mental health problems, relationship issues, and other negative consequences.

Illicit Sex

Illicit sex can lead to unintended pregnancies, STDs, emotional distress, damaged relationships, and moral degradation.
